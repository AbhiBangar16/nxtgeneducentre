Franchise Opportunity

Partner with NxtGen Edu Centre and become a part of our mission to deliver excellence in healthcare education across India.

Becoming a NxtGen Edu Centre Franchisee

Entering into a franchise partnership with NxtGen Edu Centre involves signing a formal Memorandum of Understanding (MoU). Upon signing the MoU, franchisees will gain access to a wide range of support and resources from NxtGen Edu Centre, including:

  • Authorization to use the NxtGen Edu Centre brand name and logo
  • Access to curated lectures and presentations
  • Assistance in faculty selection and training support, if required
  • Standardized teaching schedules and methodologies
  • Prescribed formats for practicals
  • Industry connections to enhance learning outcomes
  • Comprehensive guidance on examinations
  • Strong online presence and support, including website and social media platforms
  • Templates and formats for all major printed and digital marketing collaterals, such as:
    • Prospectus, brochures, flyers, and promotional folders
    • E-mailers, posters, and advertising templates
    • Signage designs and media publicity materials

Who Can Become a NxtGen Franchisee Partner?

At NxtGen Edu Centre, quality is our highest priority. We carefully select our franchise partners, seeking individuals or organizations who share our commitment to excellence in education. Ideal candidates typically possess:

  • A strong entrepreneurial mindset and drive for success
  • A long-term vision for growth and sustainability in the education sector
  • A serious commitment to healthcare education as a meaningful business endeavor
  • Adequate financial and operational resources to support the venture
